About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival roguelite game where you face endless waves of monsters. Choose your character, collect experience to level up, and select powerful abilities and weapons to enhance your survival chances. How long can you survive against the increasing monster hordes?

Game Controls

Movement:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys - Move your character
  • Mouse - Aim your attacks
  • Space - Dash (if available)
  • 1-6 - Use items

Tips:

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  • Collect experience gems to level up
  • Choose complementary abilities
  • Prioritize health and defensive upgrades

Game Features

Character Selection

Choose from multiple unique characters, each with their own starting weapons and abilities. Unlock new characters as you progress through the game and complete special challenges.

  • Unique starting weapons for each character
  • Special passive abilities
  • Different playstyles and strategies
  • Unlock system for new characters

Upgrade System

Level up and choose from a selection of weapons, passive items, and abilities. Build a powerful combination that suits your playstyle and helps you survive longer against increasingly difficult waves of monsters.

Endless Challenge

Face waves of monsters that grow stronger and more numerous as time passes. Test your skills, reflexes, and strategic thinking in this infinite survival challenge. How long can you last?

Monsters & Environments

Encounter a variety of dangerous monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns. Battle through different environments with their own challenges and obstacles. Learn to recognize monster types and adapt your strategy accordingly.
